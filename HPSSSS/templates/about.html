At MediAssist, our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge and resources needed to make informed healthcare decisions. We believe in fostering a collaborative and patient-centric approach to enhance your overall well-being. Thank you for choosing MediAssist – your partner in health.

In this project, we have implemented a chatbot that acts as your personal doctor for early detection. The chatbot is designed to provide valuable insights into your health, but it is crucial to consult with your healthcare professional for a comprehensive assessment and guidance. The chatbot's functionality includes early detection algorithms and personalized health recommendations based on user input and historical data.
